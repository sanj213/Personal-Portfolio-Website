MindPulse

Project Description

MindPulse is a comprehensive mental health platform designed to provide users with resources and support for mental well-being. The platform includes various features such as mood journaling, guided meditation, and access to professional help.

Role

As a full-stack developer, I worked on both the front-end and back-end development of the platform, ensuring a seamless user experience.

Achievements

  • Successfully launched the platform in under six months.
  • Achieved a user satisfaction rate of over 90% in feedback surveys.
  • Integrated advanced analytics to track user engagement and improve features.

Technologies Used

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and React.
